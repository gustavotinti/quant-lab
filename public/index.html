How to read this dashboard
  • Accuracy: % of historical cases (strategy trades + analogous scenarios) where the direction worked out, with the sample size n. It only becomes an order above the profile's cutoff.
  • Peak Radar: when the chart is stretched, the % tells how often a reversal came within ~21 sessions in identical episodes.
  • Orders tagged "radar": born from the Peak Radar when the classic strategies have no signal but the empirical reversal probability clears the cutoff (with smoothing — a small sample never becomes certainty). ~1-month window, always X1.
  • System scoreboard: the REAL hit rate of live-issued orders, measured when each window completes — plus the predicted-vs-realized comparison.
  • Position & leverage: fixed risk per trade; the margin already comes divided by the recommended leverage (X2 only with evidence).
  • Tap a row for details; "X-ray" opens the asset's full dossier.

Statistics derived from public data (central banks, exchanges). Not investment advice. Historical accuracy does not guarantee future results. Leverage can generate losses greater than your capital.
